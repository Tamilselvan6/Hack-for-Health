Remote Patient Monitoring

HealthAI+ facilitates real-time remote monitoring of patients' vital signs and health metrics, ensuring timely interventions and improving overall patient outcomes. Our system integrates IoT devices to securely collect and transmit data.

Key Features:

  • Real-time monitoring of vital signs such as heart rate, blood pressure, and more
  • Secure transmission of health data using state-of-the-art IoT integration
  • Early detection of potential health issues for proactive healthcare

How It Works:

  1. Patients use IoT devices to monitor and collect health data from the comfort of their homes.
  2. Data is securely transmitted to healthcare providers in real-time.
  3. Healthcare professionals analyze the data for early intervention and personalized care.
